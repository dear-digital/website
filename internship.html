Internship Guide

What you learn

We are primarily e-commerce company providing with end to end solutions for our customers. so we start with basics of webdevelopment - Front End web development, Website optimisation, SEO, Shopify/liquid. If you are already an expert then no need to worry - we have positions ranging from frontend, backend to app development and full stack for you to choose from lateron. Although some technical knowledge is a prerequisite to join, we start teaching from basics to everyone and move to some of the most advanced stuff in the e-commerce field. Your progress is based on how quickly you can learn. The primary e-commerce platform we build on is Shopify!

Internship timing

The internship will be for 3-4 months. During the internship period, you’ll have tasks assigned to you which you will have to do during the weekdays (as much as possible when the college has started) and on weekends. Specific timings and details will be provided later. The goal of tasks would be for you to eventually be able to build your own ecommerce websites. Your tasks will be reviewed, checked and concepts will be explained everyday on a pre-decided time.

Payment

The first month of the internship is Unpaid, During this time we'll check your capabilities such as discipline, ability solve problems on your own, How well you do your tasks and most importantly if you are really interested in working on what we do :) We have to let go off people those who fail these criteria after the first month, From the second month you will receive 120€( 10,000₹ approx. depending on the market conversion) stipend every month of your training period.

What next?

What next after the internship? We are a growing company and want to take on as much talent as possible. If you perform well through your internship, we’ll be happy to make you a full-time employee. With € 600 per month we offer one of the highest paying entry level salaries in India and offer remote and flexible hours. So the main goal of this internship is to properly train everyone so that they can join us as an employee ASAP.

Am I eligible?

If you are an IT or Computer student then ofcourse you are, Even if you are not then all you need is some kind of website development training: udemy, youtube or w3 is a good place to start ;) After you are done just fill the form below and we'll get in touch.
