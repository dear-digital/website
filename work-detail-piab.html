Challenge
Puzzle in a bag is an internal project that we launched during the Covid crisis. It was an instant hit. After that initial success we raised funding and spun it out of dear digital and are now building a full fledged e-commerce company from it. This is massively interesting for us to be in the dirt and learn everything the hard way.

Now we are crossing our Belgium borders and are building up our marketing efforts in Germany and France for which we have build seperate stores to really focus on these markets and tailor the shops to the local needs.
