Challenge
BRAUZZ. has one mission: creating sustainable cleaning products. Their first product was a refillable, durable bottle where you simply add a powdered concentrate together with water to create a powerful surface cleaner.

Entering a highly competitive market is hard, competing against the likes of Unilever and P&G even harder. Together we decided on targeting a niche and expanding from that niche outwards. They quickly decided to add stores so they could service that same niche in other countries. After a year they launched their now most popular product: laundry strips. We can't wait to see what's next!
