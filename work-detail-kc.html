Challenge
De Kaasconnaisseur, founded by Nick Spanhove is a cheese catering company that specializes in all-in cheese boxes. The company initially started out as a catering business for bigger parties. However, during the lockdown period in 2020 the business needed to be rethought.

We guided this transformation into a full e-commerce cheese business. De Kaasconnaisseur started out in Bruges, then expanded to neighbouring cities and nowadays it delivers in full Flanders. At the beginning of the journey there was a strong dependency on local bakeries. During the scale-up phase, we advised Nick to opt for a home delivery service, which also helped boost margin.
