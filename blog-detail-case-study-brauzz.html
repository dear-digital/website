1. Product

Last year, BRAUZZ. launched their first and flagship product: refillable and reusable spray cleaners. How does that work? Well, when you first order, you receive a refillable plastic bottle, together with a very condensed cleaning product. Put the cleaning product in the bottle, pour in some water, and huzzah! You have your very own homemade cleaner ü§©

And you know what the real magic is? You can keep refilling your bottle countless times - if you buy refills, of course üòâ This way, BRAUZZ. not only saves a lot of plastic, but also limits transport emissions by not needlessly transporting water. So, these cleaners make a brilliant idea from an ecological point of view, but BRAUZZ. is not the only player on the market offering refillable cleaners.
